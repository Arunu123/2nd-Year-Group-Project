Our Team


A new chapter in the Sri Lankan Fitness Industry started as Ironcity Gymnasium opened its doors to the public. Ironcity Gym aims to inspire active living and give you access to credible health and fitness resources and experiences all in one comprehensive platform. We collaborate with professionals across the network to curate experiences, corporate seminars, and inspirational content as well.
